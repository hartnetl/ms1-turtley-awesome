5 Ways you can help

  1. Make eco-friendly swaps. Reusable shopping bags, coffee cups, and water bottles are just a few eco-friendly alternatives you can use to help reduce plastic waste.
  2. Buy seafood from places that use sustainable fishing practices. This helps reduce levels of bycatch. While sustainable fishery screening isn't perfect, it's a start. Look out for the MSC and ASC labels.
  3. Any of these tips to keep oceans healthy, such as using less water. Don't litter, swap to eco-friendly cleaning products and recycle correctly.
  4. Organise a beach clean to keep nesting beaches clean and safe for turtles. If you don't live near a nesting beach, organise a beach clean anyway. Don't live near a beach? Organise a street or field clean!
  5. Reduce your plastic consumption! Remember to avoid products with glitter, microbeads etc. which all contribute to micro plastic pollution. If products like plastic pack rings can't be avoided, don't forget to cut them.
