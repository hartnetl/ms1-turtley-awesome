Ways you can help

  • Make eco friendly swaps. Reusable shopping bags, coffee cups and water bottles are just a few eco friendly alternatives you can use to help reduce plastic waste.
  • Buy fish from places that use sustainable fishing practices. This helps reduce levels of bycatch. While sustainable fishery screening isn't perfect, it's s start. Look out for the MSC and ASC labels.
  • Any of these tips to keep oceans healthy, such as using less water.
  • Organise a beach clean to keep nesting beaches clean and safe for turtles, or improving local environment health.
  • Remember to avoid products with glitter, microbeads etc. which all contribute to micro plastic pollution.
    Products like plastic pack rings should be cut if their use cannot be avoided.
